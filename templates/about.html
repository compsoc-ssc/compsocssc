The Computer Science Society is one of the most active student branch of St. Stephen's College. We are a group of individuals from a variety of academic backgrounds with a love for computer science. Computers are a huge part of out lives now and using them efficiently is a skill highly in demand. Unlike most societies/student groups focussed on organizing events; our primary goal is to learn new things together as a group. We organize more workshops in college than actual events and we're proud of this very fact. The CompSoc is a place for anyone with a desire and drive to learn to seek the same.
