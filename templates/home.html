About

Who are we?

The Computer Science Society (CompSoc), St. Stephen’s College is a group of individuals who believe in the idea of 'education as an enabler'. We strongly feel that keeping in mind the 21st century and how big a role technology plays in our lives, everyone should be able to program a computer. By that, we don't mean writing complex applications, no; we just want people to use their computers to their advantage!

Our Goals

CompSoc's primary goal is without a doubt education. Although, no long term goal can be acheived without a comprehensive list of intermediate short term goals. In our quest for absoulte digital litracy, we stress on enjoying onself, collaboration and exploration as well. At the end of the day, what's the fun in having no fun? ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
