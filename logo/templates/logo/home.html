Logo Designing

Welcome Logo Designers!

CompSoc has an opportunity for you to contribute your designing skills for our Annual Tech Fest- Cybercept'15. We are accepting entries for Logo of our Annual Tech Fest- Cybercept.

Cybercept is our Annually organized Tech Fest with events like Tech Hunt, Orfik - online treasure hunt, Cryptactic, Programming and X- Centricity Tech Quiz competitions. We are looking forward to have logo for our Fest, the best logo shall be used for all the publicity posters of the Fest.

It will be best if you design your logo according to the given information.

Competition is open from 17th to 25th February 2015.

To participate, kindly adhere to the following instructions:

  1. Fill up the form and all the required details.
  2. Upload your designed logo, themed on our Annual Tech Fest "Cybercept".
