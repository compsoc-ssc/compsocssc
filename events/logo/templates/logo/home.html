Welcome Logo Designers!

CompSoc has an opportunity for you to use your designing skills for our Annual Tech Fest - Cybercept. Cybercept is our Annually organized Tech Fest with events like Tech Hunt, Orfik - online treasure hunt, Cryptactic, Programming and X-Centricity - a tech quiz.

We are looking for a logo for our society's upcoming project 'e-gyaan', an initiative to familiarize junior school kids residing in economically disadvantaged localities around our college, to the basics of computers.

Rules

The competition is open from 11th to 18th March 2016. To participate, kindly adhere to the following instructions:

  1. Design a logo for society's upcoming project 'e-gyaan'.
  2. Only one entry is allowed per person. So, take your time and perfect your submission. Multiple entries could lead to disqualification.
  3. The logo should be 500x500px.
  4. Please submit a JPG or PNG file.
  5. Have fun and learn something along the way!

For more information regarding prizes or just general FAQ, head over to the facebook page.
