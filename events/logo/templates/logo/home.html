Logo Designing

Welcome Logo Designers!

CompSoc has an opportunity for you to use your designing skills for our Annual Tech Fest - Cybercept. We are accepting logo extried for Cybercept. Cybercept is our Annually organized Tech Fest with events like Tech Hunt, Orfik - online treasure hunt, Cryptactic, Programming and X-Centricity - a tech quiz. The best logo shall be used for all the publicity posters of the fest.

Rules

The competition is open from 17th to 26th February 2015. To participate, kindly adhere to the following instructions:

  1. The theme for the design is obviously Cybercept - our annual tech festival.
  2. The logo should be 500x500px.
  3. Please submit a JPG or PNG file.
  4. Have fun and learn something along the way!

For more information regarding prizes or just general FAQ, head over to http://on.fb.me/1DlVDVw
