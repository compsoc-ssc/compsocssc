Instructions

  1. The hunt begins sharp at 0000 Hours on March 17, 2016 and ends on March 18 at 23:59 Hours.
  2. Each participant can make a single login using a username of their choice.
  3. Once the game begins you will be faced by a series of questions.
  4. All answers are to be entered in lower case characters with no spaces and special characters.
  5. You can only see one question at a time and will only progress when you answer the question correctly.
  6. You can have infinite tries on each question until you get the right answer.
  7. All levels are of equal weightage. There is no negative marking.
  8. You can also see the leader board at any time to see your progress relative to others.
  9. Ranks are based on the time the question is answered; with the individual answering first placed higher.
  10. The CompSoc shall be the sole authority to decide on anything. Any decision made by CompSoc or its representatives is final and irrevocable.
  11. Any individual found to use any unfair means will be disqualified with immediate effect.
  12. The use of the internet, searching the source code etc. are all allowed to arrive at the answer.
  13. Any queries regarding the questions will be answered at the orfik forum by CompSoc on our facebook page. However, no foul language or other miscreant behaviour will be tolerated.
