Welcome Designers!

CompSoc is organising "Sell It!", a website designing competition for our Annual Tech Fest - Cybercept. Cybercept has various fun events like TechHunt, orfik - an Online Treasure Hunt, and "import this", our annual programming event.

For "Sell It!", we would like you to design the landing/front page of a website to market a new phone by Omnia. The best entry will win Rs 1000!

About Omnia

Omnia is a company that has made incredible strides in high-performance computing with its GPUs. Omnia is now venturing into the mobile phone market with a phone containing the Typhon 701.

The Typhon 701 a powerful 64-bit octa-core CPU. It supports download speeds of up to 500 Mbps and the ability to stream 4K Ultra HD video and possesses high-res digital media capabilities ensuring a cutting-edge mobile experience.

We would like you to design a website for the launch of Omnia's new phone.

Feel free to add your own ideas. Different variants, accessories, exclusive partnership deals, pre-order goodies or other aspects of the hardware. Go wild!
